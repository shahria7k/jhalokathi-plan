Revamp & Rebuild Strategy

Based on the financial analysis, here is the recommended roadmap for the Jhalakathi Municipality revamp project. These action items prioritize financial sustainability and visible infrastructure impact.

Priority 1 Revenue Automation

Observation: Holding tax collection is currently manual and inefficient (62%).
Action: Implement a GIS-based holding assessment and online payment gateway.
Goal: Increase Own Source Revenue by 25% within 2 years.

Priority 2 Expenditure Optimization

Observation: Administrative costs consume 25% of the budget.
Action: Audit non-essential posts and digitize file management to reduce overhead.
Goal: Cap establishment costs at 20%, redirecting 5% to sanitation.

Priority 3 Market Asset Management

Observation: Market rents yield only 6.1% revenue despite prime locations.
Action: Re-evaluate lease agreements based on current market rates.
Goal: Double market revenue to 12% of total income.

Priority 4 Public-Private Partnership (PPP)

Observation: Waste management is underfunded (4%).
Action: Outsource waste collection to private contractors with user fees.
Goal: Improve city cleanliness without increasing municipal headcount.
